ABOUT THE ORGANIZING COMMITTEE

BVPIEEE is the IEEE Student Branch of BVCOE. With the aim of creating a rich pool of technical experts and harnessing the boundless youth potential, we offer a wide spectrum of opportunities and resources to our members..

OUR AGENDA

To inspire girls in engineering to learn to programme so they can reverse the labor market statistics in the future. In a friendly environment, where girls will not be excluded, fun and creativity are things, that count the most.
1.Sharpen public speaking/presentation skills among women at the workplace
2.Foster teamwork
3.Learn about new technologies
4.Network and Fun!
